Our Story

iManage was founded in 1995 and merged with Interwoven in 2003. Interwoven was then acquired by Autonomy and Autonomy acquired by HP. On July 21, 2015, iManage announced a management buyout of the company from HP. The independent iManage is a market share leader rapidly gaining momentum since the buyout from HP and poised to reassert its thought leadership and expand into new markets.

Unlike simple file sharing and collaboration tools, iManage delivers content management that is purpose built for professional work and is comprehensive, intuitive, ready to go, governed and trusted.

Our mentors mean business

Your success matters to us. We pride ourselves on providing employees with the necessary tools to triumph. You can expect coaching and acknowledge hard work on a daily basis.
